The field of artificial intelligence (AI) has recently seen a substantial increase in popularity, largely fueled by the successes of training deep neural networks. In this project-based course you will work in teams to identify a need with high-impact implications on everyday life, conceptualize and design an AI system targeting this need, and develop the AI system by refining a demo-able prototype based on feedback received during course presentations.
