This course is designed to introduce advanced students to research methods and topics in human-robot interaction (HRI), an emerging research area focusing on the design and evaluation of interactions between humans and robotic technologies. Students will learn design principles for building and research methods of evaluating interactive robot systems, read and discuss relevant literature, and work on a substantial project that integrates the principles, methods, and knowledge learned in this course.
