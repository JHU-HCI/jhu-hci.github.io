Human-Centered AI Workshop (2026)

Save the date! We're organizing a one-day workshop to bring together Hopkins researchers working at the intersection of AI and human experience. We'd love for you to join!

This one-day workshop brings together researchers across Johns Hopkins to build a connected, interdisciplinary community around human-centered AI. The program will feature a keynote, lightning talks, and breakout sessions designed to surface shared challenges and encourage new collaborations.

As AI systems are increasingly deployed in real-world settings, the workshop highlights the importance of usability, safety, trustworthiness, and effective human–AI collaboration. Open to all departments, it will engage faculty from engineering, medicine, the social sciences, and beyond to ensure that advances in AI translate into meaningful benefits for people and society.

  • 📅 When: Friday, May 15, 2026
  • 📍 Where: Mt. Washington Campus – DSAI office, JHU (6225 Smith Ave, Baltimore, MD 21209)
  • 👥 Who: Primarily JHU faculty (we'll open the remaining spots to postdocs and PhD students)

Registration is mandatory, as we have limited spots!
